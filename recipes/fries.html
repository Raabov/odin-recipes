Cheesy Bacon Fries Recipe

Description

Looking for a quick cheesy snack, which you can prepare at home anytime, then try these delicious Cheesy Bacon Fries. To make these quick crispy fries just follow some simple steps given below and enjoy. You can pair these fries with some dips, homemade mocktails and serve it for your brunch parties or game nights.

To make it in a healthy way, make sure you extract the starch from the potatoes and air fry them and replace the cheesy condiments with a mixture of curd or poppy seeds dip. You can also add vegan bacon to make it more healthy.

But if want to indulge your soul in a cheesy affair, then try it in the usual style. To make these simple fries more delicious you can also prepare some homemade hot sauce and add more toppings. Try it and share your feedback with me.

Ingredients

  • 250 gm french fries
  • 1 cup cheddar cheese
  • 1 tablespoon spice oregano
  • 1/2 cup mayonnaise
  • 1 cup bacon
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • salt as required

List of Steps

  1. Fry the French Fries

    To begin with this easy recipe, take a pan and add oil to the pan. Once the pan is hot enough, add the potato sticks and fry them till they turn golden in colour. Similarly, fry the bacon strips and chop them into small bits.

  2. Make a cheesy mix

    Once the fries are golden in colour transfer them to a plate. Next, take a bowl and mix the mayonnaise with grated cheddar cheese, salt, spice oregano, paprika.

  3. Bake for 5 minutes

    Add the cheesy toppings and garnish with the bacon bits, bake for 5 minutes till the cheese melts. Serve hot with a dip and enjoy.
